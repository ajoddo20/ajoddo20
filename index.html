About

Cold Company Farm was founded in 2021, however our history started in 2017 when Jason Oddo started Bitter Ends Garden Project, a 0.9 acre plot in Verona, PA to support Bitter Ends Luncheonette, a locavore kitchen supplying a changing menu of fresh-baked breads, pastries, and sandwiches using locally grown organic vegetables. We have since expanded to a 2 acre plot with a Greenhouse. We now source a variety of vegetables to several restaurants and shops in and around the Pittsburgh area year round. All of our vegetables are grown using no pesticides and are 100% organic.
